Project Idea

Overview – The project will be a mobile app that uses the phones processors to mine data like how bitcoin (cyptocurrency) is produced but gives the users digital characters that might be of value in terms of money and the owner of the app gets the virtual coin the users produced.

Motivation – The motivation for this project is the NFT Art. Essentially the NFT (Non-Fungible token) Digital Art is becoming more like the stock market where people create digital art and sell them giving the clients full ownership of the digital art which could rise in value if the art gains recognition. NFT Digital Art that are popular today have been known to sold for more than $3,000 on average.

Description

One of the goals of this project is to take advantage of inactive phone devices and put into use, the application could be running in the background whilst it’s charging and still mining virtual coins. The main goal is to attract revenue by using virtual characters as a reward for mining virtual coins for the company and that these virtual characters could potentially be sold in the market to be made with the application. The rarity of the virtual characters will determine its value and not how much the user has mined.

Tools and technologies

The tools needed for this project will be a computer to design the application and android phones for testing and finding out how much the phone can handle. As for standard when it comes to programming coding will be needed as a tool.
